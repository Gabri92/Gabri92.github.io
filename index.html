About me

I’m a software engineer with over four years of experience, and what excites me the most is building well-crafted software and diving into new technologies that could have a real impact on my company. With a background in mechatronics and robotics, I’ve developed strong skills in math, physics, and problem-solving, which help me tackle complex challenges with a fresh perspective. Throughout my career, I’ve worked on a wide range of software projects, from automation to embedded systems, Windows apps, and even mobile applications. While robotics software and game development are my true passions, I’ve found myself constantly exploring all sorts of tech topics that catch my interest. Lately, I’ve been spending my free time learning more about web development and artificial intelligence. This has led me to pick up some foundational skills in Python, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Django, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Scikit. I’m always eager to experiment with the latest tech and keep pushing the boundaries of what I can do. I’m always open to new projects and collaborations, so don’t hesitate to reach out—especially if you share my passions and interests!
